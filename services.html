Drone Multispectral Imaging for Smarter Farming

$200/hr

Drone-based multispectral imaging gives farmers a powerful view of their fields from above. By capturing data across different light spectrums, drones can reveal plant stress, soil moisture, and nutrient variations that aren’t visible to the human eye. This insight helps optimize irrigation, fertilizer use, and crop management—boosting yield while reducing costs and environmental impact. It’s precision agriculture made simple and sustainable.

Drone Thermography for Industrial and Safety Applications

$300/hr

Drone thermography provides a fast and accurate way to detect heat anomalies and potential faults in industrial systems, energy networks, and critical infrastructure. Equipped with thermal cameras, drones can safely inspect pipelines, power lines, and buildings—identifying overheating, leaks, or insulation failures without interrupting operations. This advanced aerial monitoring improves safety, reduces downtime, and enables preventive maintenance through precise, real-time thermal analysis.

Drone Operations for Public Safety and Emergency Response

$350/hr

Drones play a vital role in supporting emergency services, law enforcement, and technical rescue teams. Equipped with high-resolution and thermal cameras, they provide rapid situational awareness during search and rescue missions, natural disasters, or hazardous incidents. By delivering real-time aerial data, drones enhance coordination, improve safety for first responders, and speed up decision-making when every second counts.
